Identity setup

  • Use a dedicated email identity for this publishing track (recommended).
  • Keep credentials in a password manager and document where they live.
  • Maintain consistent author name / imprint details across platforms.

Platform prerequisites

Amazon KDP

  • KDP account, tax interview, payout method.
  • Book metadata: title, subtitle, description, keywords, categories.
  • Final manuscript PDF and cover exports.

Heyzine

  • Upload PDF, choose theme, copy share link.

Gumroad

  • Product page copy, pricing model, attach PDF.

Substack / LinkedIn

  • Canonical post on Substack, distribution post on LinkedIn (pin it).
