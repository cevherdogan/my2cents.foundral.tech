# Foundral Substack Series Structure

## Series A — Comparative Atlases (Signature)
Purpose: Map how different strategies coexist in the same system.
Format: short context → matrix → 3–5 insights → what remains unknowable.
Cadence: occasional (1–2 per month max).

## Series B — Signals, Not Predictions
Purpose: Observe what matters without forecasting.
Format: what changed → what didn’t → what to watch.
Cadence: occasional.

## Series C — Notes on Regeneration
Purpose: personal but grounded reflections on rebuilding posture and pace.
Cadence: rare.
