# Foundral Comparative Atlas — Signature Series Rules

## Naming
Use: **Foundral Comparative Atlas — [System Name]**

## Standard Format
1) Why the system matters now
2) The actors (3–5)
3) Comparative matrix
4) What this reveals
5) What remains unknowable

## Non-ranking principle
No rankings, no predictions, no buy/sell language.
This is a map of trade-offs, roles, and dependencies.

## Where it lives
- Books: deep finite case studies
- Substack: ongoing Atlas releases
- Medium: discovery + experimentation
- Org/Ops Index: canonical reference
